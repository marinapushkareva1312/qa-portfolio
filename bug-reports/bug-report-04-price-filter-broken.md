# Bug Report #4 — Filter by Price Does Not Work

**Date:** 2026-05-31  
**Tester:** Marina Pushkareva  
**Website:** <https://academybugs.com>

-----

## Summary

The filter by price does not work on product details or product list pages — the page simply reloads without applying the filter.

-----

## Bug Details

|Field          |Value       |
|---------------|------------|
|**Severity**   |Medium      |
|**Issue Type** |Functional  |
|**Frequency**  |Every Time  |
|**Environment**|All Browsers|

-----

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Open <https://academybugs.com>
1. Click the **Find Bugs** link on the navigation bar
1. Open any product
1. On the right side menu find the **Filter by Price** section
1. Select any price range
1. Also open any item from the right side Store Menu and select price ranges on the right side menu

-----

## Expected Result

A list of products in the selected price range is shown.

-----

## Actual Result

The same page reloads without applying the selected price filter.
