# Bug Report #24 — Page Becomes Unresponsive When Clicking Retrieve Password

**Date:** 2026-05-31  
**Tester:** Marina Pushkareva  
**Website:** <https://academybugs.com>

-----

## Summary

The page becomes unresponsive when clicking the “Retrieve Password” button and no email is sent to the user.

-----

## Bug Details

|Field          |Value       |
|---------------|------------|
|**Severity**   |High        |
|**Issue Type** |Crash       |
|**Frequency**  |Every Time  |
|**Environment**|All Browsers|

-----

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Open <https://academybugs.com>
1. Click the **Find Bugs** link on the navigation bar
1. Open any product
1. Scroll down to the **Your Account** section of the right side menu
1. Click **Sign Up**
1. In the Returning Customer section click **Forgot Your Password?**
1. Enter your email in the field
1. Click **Retrieve Password**

-----

## Expected Result

The password is sent to the entered email.

-----

## Actual Result

The page becomes unresponsive when clicking “Retrieve Password” and no email is sent.
