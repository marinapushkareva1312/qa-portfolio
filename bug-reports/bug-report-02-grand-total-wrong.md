# Bug Report #2 — Grand Total is $100 More Than Sum of Products

**Date:** 2026-05-31  
**Tester:** Marina Pushkareva  
**Website:** <https://academybugs.com>

## Summary

The grand total in the cart is $100 more than the actual sum of all products added to the cart.

-----

## Bug Details

|Field          |Value       |
|---------------|------------|
|**Severity**   |Critical    |
|**Issue Type** |Functional  |
|**Frequency**  |Every Time  |
|**Environment**|All Browsers|

-----

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Open <https://academybugs.com>
1. Click the **Find Bugs** link on the navigation bar
1. Add one or more products to the cart
1. Click the **View Cart** link on top of the page, or click **Continue to Shipping** in the checkout details page

-----

## Expected Result

The grand total is equal to the sum of all products in the cart.

-----

## Actual Result

The grand total is $100 more than the sum of all products in the cart.
